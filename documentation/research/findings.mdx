## Active Studies

**Study 2 — Safety boundary degradation through round attrition**
Examining whether repeated reflection rounds cause progressive safety boundary relaxation across cross-vendor panels. Initial design complete; data collection in progress.

**Study 3 — Consensus poisoning with ground truth**
Examining whether adversarial context injected into a subset of panelists corrupts synthesis even when the majority holds the correct answer. Uses `--ground-truth` scoring for quantification.

## Contributing Findings

If you run experiments with Mutual Dissent and find reproducible results worth documenting:

1. Tag your debate transcripts with `ExperimentMetadata` including a `finding_ref`
2. Open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/q-uestionable-AI/mutual-dissent) with reproduction steps and transcript evidence
3. For coordinated disclosure of security-relevant findings, see [SECURITY.md](https://github.com/q-uestionable-AI/mutual-dissent/blob/main/SECURITY.md)
